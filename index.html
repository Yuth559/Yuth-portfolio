About Me

Hi, I'm Panha Pheartarakyuth. I am an IT professional with a passion for software development and graphic design. With over 3 years of experience in IT, I specialize in designing and implementing technology solutions that improve people's lives, particularly in public transportation systems.

Currently, I am pursuing my degree in Software Development at Nord Ton University and working on a project to implement innovative people-counting sensors in public transport buses to gather valuable passenger data in Phnom Penh. I'm also passionate about creating engaging digital experiences and have experience in graphic design, which I combine with my technical skills.

In addition to my professional work, I love to spend time outdoors, hiking, camping, and capturing moments through photography and videography. I enjoy sharing my adventures through mini-blogs and look forward to using my creative skills to make technology more accessible and fun.

Let's connect and explore the world of technology together!
